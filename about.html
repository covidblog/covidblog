About the Webpage

It all started with Covid Blog's Idea.

During the time of spread of COVID-19, there is negativity all around us. Everyone is facing very difficult time. But this time could be very productive if we want. We are always at home, and can do many things which we normally could not do due to lack of time.

It has many advantages.

This Website will help you write anything you want and show it to the world. This will not only hone your writing skills but also make everyday more enjoyable. After many years when you will forget about these time, reading your own experiences will be a very enjoyable thing.

You won't get bored again.

Here you don't only have the option to write but also you can read what others have written. You can see what others are experiencing, how others are utilizing their time, what problems they are facing and how they are solving them, and many more.
